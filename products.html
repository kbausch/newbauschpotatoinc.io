PRODUCTS

  • Russet Burbanks

    The good old classic Russet Burbank Potatoes that come in a variety of sizes and ways.

    Boxes are all 50 lbs, therefore the smaller the potatoes are, the higher the count of potatoes in the box.

    Boxes come in 5 sizes
    • 50 count (large)
    • 70 count
    • 80 count
    • 90 count
    • 100 count (small)

    Our Oversized Potatoes (sizes bigger than 50 counts) come in sacks that are 50 lbs. each.

  • Yukons
    Seasonal

    Every year we farm a few acres of Yukon potatoes to add some special variety to our potato products.

    Boxes are all 50 lbs.

  • Fries

    Fresh fries that are trimmed and ready for the fryer

      30b Box (2 sizes)
    • Crispy Fries (1/4 inch x 1/4 inch)
    • Traditional (1/4 inch x 3/8 inch)

      Box of 2 - 15lb bags
    • 3/8 inch x 3/8 inch
  • Hashbrowns

    Our Hashbrowns are freshly cut and ready for the grill.
    They are sold in 5lb. bags, most commonly sold in a 40lb. box.

      Prepared in 2 ways
    • Cut "Fine"
    • Cut "Course"
  • Diced (Cubed)

    Our Diced Potatoes are cut fresh and are perfect for pasties.
    We offer precooked 40 lb. boxes and raw 30 lb. boxes

      Prepared in 2 ways
    • 1/2 inch cubes
    • 3/4 inch cubes
  • Reds
    Seasonal

    Every year we acquire a couple acres worth of Red potatoes to add some special variety to our potato products.

    Sacks are all 50 lbs.

  • Sliced

    We slice the potatos into thin slices, perfect for making homemade chips!

    Boxes are all 40 lbs.

  • Peeled

    If you don't have the time to peel your own potatoes then we can peel them fresh for you!

    Boxes are 40 lbs.
